Information We Collect

  • Your Facebook public profile information (name, profile picture)

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your MyLinked account
  • Display your profile information on your public MyLinked page
  • Provide authentication and account management services
  • Improve our service and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Facebook Data

When you connect your Facebook account, we only access basic profile information (name, profile picture) that you've made publicly available. We comply with Facebook's Platform Policy and data use requirements.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support system within the MyLinked application.
